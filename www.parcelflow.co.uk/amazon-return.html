Introducing Amazon Partners For Returns & Distribution
Go to Services

Our new service allows non UK based companies who sell on Amazon to use our depot to save on shipping costs.

So how does it work? Parcel Flow can accept your returns in the UK of your products and ship large quantity orders back in one to your business saving you on shipping costs and hassle of tracking individual returns.

And we also do this for your sales, all you need is to ship a consignment to us and when your orders flood in you notify what, who and where to send them again saving on shipping.

All designed with costs and convenience in mind for you.
